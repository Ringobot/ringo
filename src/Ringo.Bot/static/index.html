Ringo

Welcome to the Ringo bot preview! You can chat with Ringo on Slack. Microsoft Teams is coming soon.

Ringo is just a baby; it essentially only understands two things: artists you like and artists that you want to listen to, e.g. 

I like Madonna

When you like an Artist, Ringo will recommend another one to you, taking you on a journey of discovery. Behind the scenes Ringo is recording your likes and using them (and the likes of others) to make better recommendations to you. 

Play Madonna

If you have a Spotify Premium account you can authorize Ringo to play you music on Spotify (the music plays on your device).

As you preview Ringo we would love your feedback. What would you like Ringo to understand? What would you like Ringo to do?
